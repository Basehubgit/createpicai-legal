Delete your CreatePicAI account

Effective date: June 1, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your CreatePicAI account and the personal data associated with it. CreatePicAI is the AI Poster & Avatar Creator mobile application operated by an independent developer (contact: sepbat8@gmail.com).

Two ways to delete

Option A — Delete individual content without deleting your account

You can remove items from your generation history at any time without deleting your account:

  1. Open the CreatePicAI app and go to the History tab.
  2. Tap any generation you want to remove.
  3. In the detail view, tap the red Delete button.
  4. The prompt and image URL are removed from your account immediately on our servers and on your device.

Option B — Delete your entire account

To request full account deletion, send an email to sepbat8@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: CreatePicAI Account Deletion Request
  • Email body: the Google account email address you used to sign in to CreatePicAI.

We confirm the request and complete the deletion within 30 days of receiving it (typically within 24–48 hours). We will email a confirmation when deletion is complete.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is removed from our systems:

  • Your Google account identifier, email address, display name and profile picture URL.
  • Your remaining credit balance.
  • Your complete generation history (all prompts and image URLs you have produced through the app).

What is retained (and for how long)

  • Anonymized request logs (such as HTTP status codes and timestamps without user identifiers) may be retained for up to 30 days in our backend hosting provider's log system for security and abuse-prevention purposes, after which they are rotated and discarded.
  • No personal information is retained after account deletion is processed.
  • Data on third-party AI providers (Replicate) follows their own retention policy and is not under our direct control. The images Replicate generates are produced on demand and are not stored long-term by Replicate.

App-side data

Locally stored data on your device (cached history, theme, language preference, sign-in token) is removed when you uninstall the CreatePicAI app or tap Sign out in the Profile tab.

Contact

If you have any question about this process, please contact us at sepbat8@gmail.com.
