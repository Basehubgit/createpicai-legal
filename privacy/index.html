Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the CreatePicAI mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are

CreatePicAI is an AI-powered image generation mobile application developed and operated by an independent developer. You can contact us at deepthorin@gmail.com.

2. Information we process

CreatePicAI is designed to be privacy-friendly and local-first. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other directly identifying information.

The following information is processed when you use the App:

  • Prompts you type (text describing the image you want). Prompts are sent to our backend proxy and forwarded to our AI provider to generate the image.
  • Reference images you upload (optional, image-to-image feature). The image is sent to our backend proxy and forwarded to the AI provider. We do not store these images.
  • Generated images. Returned by the AI provider and shown in the App. URLs to generated images are temporarily cached by your device and may be stored in the App's local history on your device.
  • Local app state (selected language, theme, credit balance, history of your prompts and generated images, onboarding status). This data is stored only on your device using the operating system's local storage and never leaves your device unless you choose to share content.
  • Standard request metadata processed by our hosting provider (Render) to deliver the service, such as IP address, request timestamp, and basic HTTP headers. This data is processed only to operate, secure, and debug the service and is not used to build a profile of you.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, phone, address, or government IDs.
  • Your contacts, calendar, or location.
  • Photos from your gallery — only the specific image you choose to upload is processed.
  • Advertising identifiers; the App does not show ads and does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

3. Third-party processors

To deliver the core feature of the App (AI image generation), we use the following third-party services:

  • Replicate, Inc. — hosts the AI image generation models (Flux 1.1 Pro and Flux 1.1 Pro Ultra by Black Forest Labs). Your prompt and any reference image you upload are sent to Replicate for the sole purpose of generating the requested image. See Replicate's privacy policy: https://replicate.com/privacy.
  • Render Services, Inc. — hosts our backend proxy that securely forwards requests to Replicate without exposing API credentials on your device. See Render's privacy policy: https://render.com/privacy.

For non-English prompts, the prompt is translated to English before being passed to the image model, using a language model hosted on Replicate. The translation is performed for image generation purposes only and is not stored.

4. How long we keep data

  • On your device: until you delete the data from within the App or uninstall the App.
  • On our backend: we do not maintain a database of user prompts or generated images. Prompts and image references are processed in transit and not persisted on our servers beyond standard request logs, which are kept for a short period for security and debugging and are then rotated.
  • At Replicate: retention is governed by Replicate's own policies, linked above.

5. Children

CreatePicAI is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the App in a way that has resulted in data being processed, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Security

All network requests between the App and our backend, and between our backend and Replicate, are made over HTTPS. API credentials for Replicate are stored only on our backend and are never embedded in the mobile App.

7. Your rights

Because the App stores most data locally on your device and does not associate data with a personal identifier, the most effective ways to exercise your rights are to:

  • Delete individual items from your history inside the App.
  • Uninstall the App, which removes all locally stored data.
  • Contact us at deepthorin@gmail.com with any question regarding your data. If you are located in the EU/EEA, UK, or California, you have the right to ask whether we process personal data relating to you, to request deletion, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. AI-generated content

Images created with the App are generated by an AI model based on your prompt. You can use the in-app Report content button to flag any generated content you believe violates our content policy (sexual content involving minors, hate or violent content targeting protected groups, copyright infringement, or other concerns). Reports help us improve safety.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated inside the App where reasonable.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at deepthorin@gmail.com.
